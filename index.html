Welcome to DH Sports Camp

At DH Sports Camp, we're passionate about sports, teamwork, and fun! Our day camp (for ages 8-12) provides a supportive and energetic environment to learn new skills, play their favorite games, and build character. We focus on sportsmanship and effort, with a dash of creative arts for a well-rounded camp experience.

A Day Full of Action

Team Sports

Learn and play favorites like soccer, basketball, and flag football in a friendly setting.

Creative Arts & Crafts

A perfect break from the action to paint, draw, and build cool projects.

Skill-Building Drills

Fun games and drills to improve coordination, teamwork, and confidence.
